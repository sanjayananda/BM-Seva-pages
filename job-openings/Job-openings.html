What to expect from the life at the center

By joining the team in Shree Peetha Nilaya, one not only gets a "job", but also joins a family of like minded spiritual seekers from all over the world, who have devoted their life to the search for God through the "Bhakti" path, as well as the service to humanity.

A working day begins with prayers in the morning and concludes with evening prayers. Working and living in the centre is therefore suitable for all those who aim to make God the focal point in their lives.
